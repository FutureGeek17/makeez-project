About Makeez

Welcome to Makeez—Dubai's ultimate late-night kitchen for flavor seekers in Al Khawaneej and Za’abeel. Operating from 1 PM to the early hours, our no-frills, delivery-first concept brings gourmet-style burgers, juicy steaks, loaded fries, and fusion noodles right to your door.

Our crew is passionate about serving bold, crave-worthy comfort food made with care. From our famous Buffalo Chicken Sliders and Truffle Burger to cheesy Korean noodles and decadent desserts, every dish is designed to delight.

Makeez is the go-to spot for those midnight munchies. We combine top-quality ingredients, vibrant flavors, and seamless delivery to redefine fast food—no dine-in, no fuss, just flavor.

Craving something unforgettable? Order now and taste the Makeez difference.
